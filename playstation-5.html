PS5 Repair Services in Nagpur

Akku Electronics offers specialized repair services for both the PlayStation 5 Digital Edition and Disc Edition. As the latest console from Sony, the PS5 features advanced hardware that requires expert handling for repair. Whether it's a common issue or something more complex, we're here to help.

Common PlayStation 5 Repair Issues:

  • HDMI Port Repair: The most frequent repair for PS5, causing no signal to the TV. We provide precise HDMI port replacements.
  • Disc Drive Repair: For the Disc Edition, issues with games not reading, discs getting stuck, or grinding noises.
  • No Power / Intermittent Power: Console failing to turn on, turning off unexpectedly, or suffering from power supply unit (PSU) faults.
  • Loud Fan Noise / Overheating: Excessive fan noise, coil whine, or system shutdown due to overheating. Cleaning and thermal paste replacement can often resolve this.
  • Wi-Fi / Bluetooth Module Issues: Problems connecting to the internet, slow download speeds, or controller connectivity issues.
  • APU (CPU/GPU) Issues: More complex problems leading to graphic distortions, crashes, or complete system failure (often related to 'white light' symptoms).
  • USB Port Repair: Damaged USB-A or USB-C ports affecting charging or peripheral connectivity.

Our technicians are trained on the latest console technology, ensuring that your PS5 receives the highest quality care. We use genuine or high-grade compatible parts for all repairs, aiming to restore your console to its peak performance.
