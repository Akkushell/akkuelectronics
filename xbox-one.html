At Akku Electronics, we handle all hardware and software issues for the Xbox One family. Whether you have the original model, the sleek One S, or the powerful One X, our repair services cover everything from common faults to complex electronic issues.

Repair Your Xbox One

Common Xbox One Repair Issues:

  • HDMI Port and No Video Output: Repairing physically damaged HDMI ports or fixing internal chips to restore video.
  • Disc Drive Failure: Fixing drives that won't read discs, make grinding noises, or fail to eject.
  • No Power or Flashing Light: Diagnosing and repairing issues with the internal or external power supply.
  • Loud Fan and Overheating: Internal cleaning, thermal paste replacement, and fan repairs to prevent overheating.
  • Storage & OS Errors: Fixing boot-up issues, E-series error codes, and replacing failed hard drives.
  • Controller Sync & Connectivity Issues: Repairing Wi-Fi and Bluetooth modules for connectivity problems.

We use high-quality replacement parts and provide a warranty on all our repairs. Trust our experienced technicians to get your Xbox One working perfectly again.
