Common PlayStation Problems We Fix

No Power / Won't Turn On

Issues with power supply units, internal components, or sudden shutdowns.

HDMI Port Repair

No video output or distorted display due to damaged HDMI ports.

Overheating / Fan Noise

Excessive heat, loud fan noises, or console shutting down to prevent damage.

Disc Drive Errors

Console not reading discs, ejecting discs, or making grinding noises.

Network / Wi-Fi Connectivity

Problems connecting to Wi-Fi, Ethernet, or maintaining stable online connection.

Specific Error Codes (BLOD/YLOD)

Diagnosing and fixing "Blue Light of Death" (PS4) or "Yellow Light of Death" (PS3) errors.

Storage / Hard Drive Issues

Corrupted data, failing hard drives, slow loading, or storage upgrades.

Controller Pairing / Charging

Issues with controllers not connecting, charging, or unexpected disconnections.
