At Akku Electronics, we specialize in the repair of all Xbox 360 models. The Xbox 360 can suffer from various hardware and software problems due to age, use, and design flaws.

Xbox 360 Repair

Common Xbox 360 Repair Issues:

  • Red Ring of Death (RROD): The most common fatal error. We perform advanced board-level repairs to fix the hardware failure causing the three flashing red lights.
  • Disc Read Errors: Repair or replacement of the optical drive when games are not loading, discs are scratched, or the tray won't open.
  • Power Supply Issues: Fixing issues where the console won't power on, or the external power brick is showing a wrong color light.
  • Overheating Problems: Cleaning, thermal paste replacement, and fan repairs to prevent overheating.
  • Hard Drive & Storage: Troubleshooting hard drive failure, data corruption, or installing a replacement/upgraded drive.
  • AV/HDMI Port Issues: Repairing damaged video ports to restore picture and sound quality.

Our experienced technicians use high-quality tools and parts to ensure your console is repaired correctly and lasts longer. We offer a fast turnaround time and a detailed diagnosis before any repair begins.
