Frequently Asked Questions

When is GTA 6 coming to PC?

Rockstar Games has announced that GTA 6 will come to PC, but no official release date has been confirmed. Industry sources suggest a 2025-2026 window, but this is speculation. An official announcement is expected soon.

Is GTA 6 available on PlayStation 4 or Xbox One?

No. GTA 6 is exclusive to PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X/S. The game requires next-generation hardware and will not run on PS4 or Xbox One due to hardware limitations.

How long is the campaign?

The main campaign is estimated to take 30-40 hours to complete the primary story. However, with side activities, optional missions, and exploration, the total playtime can exceed 150+ hours.

Is GTA 6 single-player only?

GTA 6 features a rich single-player campaign, but online multiplayer features and GTA Online components are available for players with internet connectivity. The single-player experience can be enjoyed fully offline.

Can I transfer my GTA Online character?

GTA 6 features a new GTA Online experience with fresh characters and progression. Legacy character transfers from GTA V are not supported, though Rockstar is exploring integration options.

What storage do I need?

GTA 6 requires approximately 140-150 GB of storage space. An SSD is essential for installation. External SSD expansion is supported on both PS5 and Xbox Series X/S.

Does GTA 6 support 120 FPS?

Yes. GTA 6 offers a 120 FPS performance mode on both PS5 and Xbox Series X/S, provided you have a compatible 120Hz display. Standard and performance modes are available with different visual settings.

Are there microtransactions in the campaign?

The single-player campaign has no mandatory microtransactions. GTA+ subscription and GTA Online features include optional cosmetic purchases, but the main story is complete without spending additional money.

What's the age rating for GTA 6?

GTA 6 is rated M for Mature (17+) by the ESRB, 18 by PEGI, and 18 by USK. The game contains violence, strong language, and adult themes. It's intended for mature audiences only.
