# Creative Shot List (Match Each Post)

Use this to quickly shoot images/reels for the 14-day posts.

## Photo/Reel Checklist
- Clean table + good lighting
- Close-up before/after shots
- One logo frame (branding)
- One final working test frame on screen
- Vertical version for reels/stories

## Day-wise Creative Guide
1. PS5 HDMI: damaged port close-up + TV output after repair
2. Controller drift: stick movement test + repaired gameplay clip
3. Xbox no power: console not turning on + successful boot clip
4. Cleaning: dust removal before/after fan noise clip
5. Trust post: packaged console ready for delivery
6. Firmware promo: screenshot of firmware page on phone/laptop
7. Weekend slots: simple poster with “limited slots”
8. PS4 focus: 3-image collage (HDMI, fan cleaning, disc test)
9. Xbox Series focus: bench shot + successful display output
10. Shop promo: product flat lay (controllers/accessories)
11. HDMI tip: cable handling demo (correct vs wrong)
12. Blog promo: screenshot of blog page + article thumbnails
13. Quote funnel: text card with 3 required details (model/issue/video)
14. Recap: collage of week’s repairs + thank-you text
