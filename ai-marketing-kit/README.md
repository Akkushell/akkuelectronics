# AI Marketing Kit - Akku Electronics

This folder contains ready-to-use social promotion assets for your website and services.

## What is included

1. `30-day-content-calendar.csv`
   - 30 days of planned posts
   - Platform-wise mapping (Facebook, Instagram, X)
   - Post format, CTA, and goal

2. `ready-posts.md`
   - Ready captions for daily posting
   - Facebook + Instagram + X variants
   - Local business tone for Nagpur audience

3. `hashtags.md`
   - Platform-specific hashtag packs
   - Service-specific tags (PS5, PS4, Xbox, HDMI, etc.)

4. `ad-copy-variations.md`
   - Paid ad text variations
   - Awareness, lead-gen, and conversion angles

5. `dm-reply-templates.md`
   - Quick replies for inbox/WhatsApp/social DMs
   - Quote request, timeline, pricing, pickup/drop flow

6. `ai-prompts.md`
   - Reusable prompts to generate new posts with AI
   - Prompts for reels, carousels, threads, and offers

7. `monthly-tracking-sheet.csv`
   - KPI tracking template for social + website outcomes

## Usage workflow (daily)

1. Open `30-day-content-calendar.csv`
2. Copy corresponding caption from `ready-posts.md`
3. Add hashtags from `hashtags.md`
4. Post via Meta Business Suite + X scheduler
5. Track results weekly in `monthly-tracking-sheet.csv`

## Suggested posting schedule

- Facebook: 5 posts/week
- Instagram: 5 posts/week + 2 reels/week
- X (Twitter): 1-2 posts/day
