PlayStation 3 Repair

Common PlayStation 3 Repair Issues:

  • Yellow Light of Death (YLOD): The most notorious PS3 issue, often caused by solder joint failure due to overheating. We provide professional reflow and reballing services to fix this problem.
  • Disc Drive Failure: The Blu-ray drive stops reading discs or fails to eject. We can replace or repair the drive mechanism and laser.
  • No Power: The console will not turn on. This is often related to a faulty power supply unit (PSU).
  • Hard Drive Failure: Corrupted data, boot loops, or error messages. We can replace the hard drive and reinstall the system software.
  • Wi-Fi / Bluetooth Module Issues: Loss of wireless connectivity to the internet or controllers.

Our experienced technicians use high-quality tools and parts to ensure your console is repaired correctly and lasts longer. We offer a fast turnaround time and a detailed diagnosis before any repair begins.
